Preparation for Mantra Chanting

To maximize the benefits of chanting, follow these steps:

  • Choose a quiet and clean space.
  • Wear comfortable and clean clothing.
  • Face east or north for positive energy flow.
  • Light a lamp or incense to create a sacred atmosphere.
  • Sit in a comfortable position with a straight spine.
  • Use a mala (prayer beads) if available to keep count.
  • Chant with devotion and clear pronunciation.
  • Maintain a calm and meditative state throughout.
  • After chanting, sit in silence for a few minutes to absorb the vibrations.
